About Me

My name's Jacob, and I'm an aspiring front-end/mobile developer. I'm a junior at the University of Washington in Seattle studying Informatics with a concentration in Human-Computer Interaction, and currently teach part-time at Computing Kids, teaching students the fundamentals of computer science. I love creating websites and apps that are both useful and enjoyable to use, keeping up with development trends to ensure they are as optimized as possible. In my free time, I also enjoy doing photography, videography, and playing the violin.
